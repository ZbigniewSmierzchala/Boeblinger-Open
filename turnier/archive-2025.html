41. Böblinger Open Tournament

41. Böblinger Open

26.12.2025 - 30.12.2025 • Böblingen

Tournament Review

Turnierrückblick

Between Christmas and New Year, 251 participants from 14 countries gathered for the 41st Böblingen Open and competed over nine rounds for prize money as well as ELO and DWZ rating points. Of the 251 participants, 19 were female. Among them were the International Women Masters Hanne Goosens from Belgium and Lina Nassr from Algeria, who have the strength to challenge one or another of the tournament favorites. The longest journeys were made by Micah Young and Lachlan Wang from Australia. India was also strongly represented. The mostly young players often traveled with their families and coaches. All of them aspire to follow in the footsteps of World Chess Champion Dommaraju Gukesh and former World Champion Viswanathan Anand, who are revered like pop stars in their home country. Fifteen local players from the two Böblingen clubs, Schachclub and Sportvereinigung, aimed to make use of their home advantage.
